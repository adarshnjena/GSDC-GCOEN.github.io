FAQS

  What is 30 Days of Google Cloud?

30 Days of Google Cloud program will provide you an opportunity to kickstart your career in cloud and get hands-on practice on Google Cloud Platform - the tool that powers apps like Google Search, Gmail and YouTube.

  What is Qwiklabs?

Qwiklabs is a platform where you can learn various Cloud technologies on GCP and AWS.

  Do I need to pay or buy anything?

No, the course is free and you are provided with 600 credits to complete our tracks in Qwiklabs.

  I completed one track, what should I do?

You may or may not learn another track (Up to you).

  I have completed the required tracks and/or skill badges. When will I get my prizes?

If you have completed any of the tracks mentioned in the Prize Rules section and are eligible for prizes, then you will get them delivered to you after the program is finished.

  What are other benefits I get?

Upon completing each quest you get a digital badge, certifying you that you are proficient in that domain of GCP.

  I have already completed the skill badges in the program, what should I do?

Please note that in order to get the prizes, you need to complete the skill badges after 27th September 2020 and before 27th October 2021 11.59 PM IST. Any quests completed before that won't be counted.
